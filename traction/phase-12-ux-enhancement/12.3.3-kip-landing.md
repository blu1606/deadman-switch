## 1. The "Active Hero" (Paper Mario Style)
**Status:** P1 (Hackathon)
**Concept:** "Fake 3D" using 2D sprites with CSS parallax.
-   **Component:** `PaperMarioKip.tsx`.
-   **Tech:**
    -   CSS `perspective: 1000px`.
    -   Body: `translateZ(0px)`.
    -   Shadow: `rotateX(60deg) translateZ(-10px) blur(4px)`.
    -   Animation: Floating up/down independently.

## 2. The "Reactive Companion"
**Status:** P1 (Hackathon)
**Concept:** Kip reacts to user cursor/intent.
-   **Hovers:**
    -   Hover "Secure My Legacy": Kip **Jumps** / **Thumbs Up**.
    -   Hover "Problem Section": Kip **Hides Eyes** / **Sad**.
-   **Tech:** Global cursor tracking or Group Hover states.

## 3. The "Storyteller" (Dynamic Island)
**Status:** P1 (Hackathon)
**Concept:** Mobile-first "Top Pill" notification.
-   **Component:** `StoryIsland.tsx`.
-   **UI:** Black pill at top-center (`fixed`).
-   **Behavior:** Collapsed (Avatar) -> Expands to text on scroll.
-   **Script:**
    -   `0%`: "Hi! I'm Kip. I'll keep your secrets safe."
    -   `30%`: "If you go offline... who handles this?"
    -   `60%`: "I check your heartbeat every 30 days."
    -   `90%`: "Let's build your vault now!"
